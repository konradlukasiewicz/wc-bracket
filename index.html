Rules & Scoring

More league rules will be added here. Here is how points work for now:

Group stage

You earn 200 points for every team you place in its exact finishing position (1st, 2nd, 3rd, or 4th) within a group, plus a 200-point bonus for any group whose entire 1-to-4 order you predict correctly.

Knockout

You earn points for every team you correctly predict to reach a given round:

Reaches Round of 32100
Reaches Round of 16200
Reaches Quarterfinal400
Reaches Semifinal800
Reaches Final1600
Wins the tournament3200

These stack. A team you take all the way to the title earned points at every round it survived, so deep correct picks are worth the most. The third-place playoff is ignored.
